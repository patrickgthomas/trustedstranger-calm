Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trusted Stranger – Calm?
Trusted Stranger – Calm is a simple daily meditation, breathing, reflection, and color-noise app designed to help create a calmer daily routine.
Do I need an account?
No. The core app experience does not require an account or login.
Does the app work offline?
Yes. Daily content, favorites, settings, and the color noise generator are designed to work locally on your device.
What is the Color Noise Generator?
The Color Noise Generator lets you play different steady background sounds, including white, pink, brown, blue, purple, gray, and green noise.
Can I save favorites?
Yes. You can save favorite meditations, affirmations, breathing exercises, and reflection prompts.
Can I set reminders?
Yes. You can enable daily reminders and choose a preferred reminder time in the app settings.
